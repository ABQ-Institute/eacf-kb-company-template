# Company

Company-level knowledge: identity, structure, strategy, policies, brand, decisions, and how the organisation works.

**Scope:** Company-wide context. Individual team members belong in their department's `3-team.md`, not here.

| Section | Content |
|---------|---------|
| `1-identity/` | Mission, vision, values, positioning |
| `2-structure/` | Org chart + management layer only (not a full employee list) |
| `3-strategy/` | Current focus, OKRs, roadmap |
| `4-policies/` | Communication, security, finance policies |
| `5-brand/` | Voice & tone, visual identity |
| `6-decisions/` | Company-level Architecture Decision Records |
| `7-team/` | How the whole organisation works: tools, AI workflow, ways of working |
